### Input

a number

### Output

```
        Even number: 10
        Odd number: 9
        Even number: 8
        Odd number: 7
        Even number: 6
        Odd number: 5
        Even number: 4
        Odd number: 3
        Even number: 2
        Odd number: 1
```

    print(n)
